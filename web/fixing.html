Getting Started Fixing Polygons in OSM

There are many ways of helping with this effort depending on your skill level and experience with OSM.

Maproulette

We have created several Maproulette challenges that even a beginner can use.

Currently there is only one challenge available. We will provide more challenges in the future, but wanted to start out with a simple challenge that is easy to create (for us) and has problems that are easy to fix (for you). Once we gain some experience with the whole process, other challenges will follow.

Self-Intersecting Building Outlines

Difficulty level: Easy

Challenge started: 2017-02-14

Description: Building outlines made from a single way that intersects itself. Self-intersection creates invalid polygons that should be fixed. Sometimes these are not rendered. Only ways with less than 100 nodes show up.

To fix this: Remove the self-intersection. Use the aerial images to verify that there is a building. Often the intersection is due to a node near (or directly on top of) another node at one of the corners and you can simply merge those nodes.

The challenge has been split up very roughly by region:

  • Africa
  • Asia
  • Australia
  • Europe
  • India
  • North America
  • South America

OSM Inspector

The Areas View in the OSM Inspector show a lot of different information about broken areas. It is a great tool for the advanced mapper, but can be daunting for the beginner. Old-style multipolygons do not show up in this view, because it would be too much data.
